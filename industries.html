Why niche matters

The campaign has to match how the customer buys.

Some buyers compare project photos. Some want the fastest pickup. Some need proof that the surface will not be damaged. The ad, landing page, and follow-up path should respect that.

Different urgency

Emergency cleanouts, seasonal cleanup, and planned patios all move at different speeds.

Different proof

Before-and-after photos, project value, surface safety, and speed matter differently by trade.

Different qualification

A good form asks enough to qualify without killing intent.
