Cost drivers

Do not compare lead cost without looking at job value.

The same cost per lead can be strong for hardscaping and weak for a low-ticket one-time job. The audit starts with margin and close rate before scaling spend.

Good budget fit

  • The target service has enough value to support ads.
  • The owner can answer leads fast and track estimate outcomes.
  • The first test is focused on a service with real demand.

Weak budget fit

  • The job value is too low for paid acquisition.
  • Leads sit unanswered until they go cold.
  • The owner wants guaranteed jobs before any campaign data exists.
