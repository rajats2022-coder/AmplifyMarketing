First 60 days

The first 60 days should create clarity, not fake certainty.

A responsible Meta ads test should find the services, areas, forms, and follow-up paths that can create booked opportunities.

Early goal

Learn what can scale, what needs fixing, and what should be cut before spend gets bigger.

Timeline

Each phase has a different job.

The first month should clean the account and lead path. The second month should push budget toward the angles with better lead quality.

Week 1

Audit and setup

Map services, areas, lead form questions, tracking, and the first campaign angle.

Weeks 2-3

Launch and watch

Read early lead quality, follow-up speed, bad-fit reasons, and form friction.

Weeks 4-6

Revise the weak signals

Adjust creative, forms, service areas, and offers that are creating waste.

Weeks 7-8

Scale or rebuild

Move attention toward campaigns creating booked opportunities and revenue notes.
