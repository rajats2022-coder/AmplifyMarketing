Good fit

  • You know the service lines you want more of.
  • You can answer leads quickly and track estimate outcomes.
  • You have enough margin to test Meta ads responsibly.

Not a fit yet

  • You need guaranteed jobs before any campaign data exists.
  • You cannot respond to new leads within the same day.
  • The target job value is too low to support ads and management.
